QUEENSLANDER GRILL

RESIDENTIAL

"An elegant clean-lined modern environment to boost creativity and showcase their prized possessions."

CONCEPT

The Queenslander Grill is an Australian Restaurant located in Richmond, Virginia. The style is a mixture of the Australian Queenslander and urban contemporary. Timber and iron exposed high pitched beams, clean lines, crisp cloths, and the cool open veranda will create a fine dining experience. A cool grey color pallet and a wall filled with different wines from the many regions of Australia will be showcased before you enter the dining area. The floor to ceiling movable frosted glass walls will divide the space for privacy and private events. Adorned with adjustable swinging chandeliers to give a warm elegant feeling.
