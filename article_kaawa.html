KAAWA RESIDENCES

RESIDENTIAL

"An elegant clean-lined modern environment to boost creativity and showcase their prized possessions."

CONCEPT

Different tones of lighting will give the piece of art life and beauty. While neutral wall tones will act as a blank canvas to the artistic mediums. The different stones used will provide different textures to the walls. The floors will be of warm dark and light earth tones not taking away from the beauty of the art. There will be large floor to ceiling windows to provide lots of natural light with UV protection from the sun. Showcasing a large floor to ceiling brick and glass wine cellar to entertain their guest. Giving the individual a feeling as if they are in a personal and private art gallery nestled in the Florida Keys.

PROBLEM

The Kaawas are an artistic couple who love wine and art. They are moving from Hawaii, and also have great love for food. Mrs. Kaawa is a painter and Mr. Kaawa is a Creative Director for one of the Top Businesses in the Graphic Design Industry. Therefore, he is able to work remote and will need to have a space for him to work and do a teleconference. They have asked Flormina Designs to design their brand new home in Key Largo, Florida. They want their home to be warm and inviting just like their hearts.

CONCLUSION

The Kawwa Residence is designed to cater to their hosting and home office needs. The room by the master bedroom is his home office. He is able to accommodate his clients, and is able to relax with a day bed to enjoy the outside views. The main floor is easy to maneuver around for a wheel chair and the kitchen has three different levels of counter space. Also, the kitchen has a custom walk-in glass wine cellar to hold most of their wine collection. The upper level of the home has two bedrooms and a kitchenette. The 2nd bedroom with the vaulted ceiling is Mrs. Kaawa’s design studio, and the 3rd bedroom is a guest bedroom for guest that wish to spend time with the Kaawa’s.
