ARC HOMES

COMMERICAL

"Diving through the depths of the everyday flow."

CONCEPT

A easy walk through the design world showcasing the worker bees. Releasing unnecessary barriers, and keeping the mind focused on the design solutions. The world around them constantly moving and growing as the space nurtures their every need.

PROBLEM

Arc homes wants an office that will best fit their needs by making everything readily accessible. With a child’s room for the employee’s kids that are out of school or need to be in close proximity. They want their teams to be able to communicate openly so everyone can know what is happening. The managers get private offices and three conference rooms to conduct client, vendor, construction, and learning meetings.

CONCLUSION

The space is very open with a lecture hall with a conference table. The design library is close to the reception area for easy access to the vendors or reps that need to update some samples or other material. The kids room is close to the kitchen on the first floor so the works would be able to concentrate on work upstairs. The second level all teams are able to work together without having to move too far. Close to the managers. The principal’s office is large with an executive desk sofa, armchairs, and modern shelving.
