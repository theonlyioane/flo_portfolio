CARYLYLE HOTEL

HOSPITALITY

"Beach chic with splashes of Art-Deco flare."

CONCEPT

The business individual or traveler on dime is the traveler in mind. Tending to the world's changing lifestyles. Illuminating the space around them. Growing on iridescent surfaces to spark their interest to find they have to look no farther.

PROBLEM

The Carlyle Hotel wants to rejuvenate their interior spaces. Their building is part of the Historical Landmarks of Miami, and the plans must be accepted by the society in order to win the bid. They would like their guests to feel welcomed and relaxed during their stay or visit.

CONCLUSION

The Carlyle Hotel will create a relaxing experience for its visitors young and young at heart and will. While tending to their needs to the best of their advantages. The light blues, greys, whites and geometric designed walls creates a vibe of business, fun, and sophistication. While most of the furnishings is made up of sustainable materials. The frosted glass sliding partition wall creates privacy when closed. The circular tables in the lobby lounge area have electrical and usb sockets for charging. The different floor textures help create a guide for the visitors walk through.
