CONCEPT

Centered on Egypt’s history and cultural background, the Nationality Room portrays the harmony of their culture and traditions with the Gods. Tranquil with a vast amount of colorful earth tones to lift the spirits up to “Nut” the goddess of the sky. The Eye of Horus will serve as a portal from our world to the Ancient world granting the users and visitors safe passage through the tomb.

Colossal colorful sand stone lotus columns will adorn the walls. Engraved with important dates and periods of Egypt’s history in hieroglyphics and Arabic writing. As the back wall will have a cool, turquoise water panel treatment symbolizing rejuvenation from the Nile. The window coverings will resemble papyrus scrolls, dating back to 500 B.C. While the white board will be enclosed by 2 tablet relics found in Pharaohs tombs. Seating will consist of stone carved sphinxes on the arms of the seat, with vibrant colorful cushions. And the floors will resemble the sand colored stone the pyramids.
